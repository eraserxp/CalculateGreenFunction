\subsection{Detailed Description}
\doxyref{Interaction\-Data}{p.}{structInteractionData} stores the onsite energy, the strengths of hopping and dynamic interactions. It also specifies whether the interactions are random or not, and contains the seed for generating the random interactions and the effective range of hopping and dynamic interactions
